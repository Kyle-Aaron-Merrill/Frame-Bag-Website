Warranty Policy

We make high quality frame bags but, accidents happen. If there is a problem with your pack we provide warranties only for defects. This means no wear or tear, zipper wear, tire rub, or improper mounting. Customers who do not qualify for a warranty are still edible for bag repair. Bag repair pricing may change depending on the damage to the product. For a warranty or repair please fill out the warranty form below.
