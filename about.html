About US

We are a shop based out of Rockford MI. We have been designing frame bags since 2017. We experiment with all types of designs and fabric. We experiment with handlebar and seat bag designs as well. We test all of our bags in the field on a yearly basis. If there is a problem with fabric or stitching we will find it as these bags are taken through thick woods, sand, and even rivers. We devote our time and research to developing the most simple, cost effective, earth proof bags. We ensure that you will enjoy your equipment without breaking the bank.

We build bags around your frame. This means we exclude areas where straps may interfere with any other equipment, frame housing stops, and water bottle mounts. We use cardboard to sketch out rough designs before transferring them to the fabric. Any bag that fails to match the frame spec can be returned for redesigning.

We are always experimenting with designs and fabric. Whether it is an attachment system, lightweight fabric, colorful fabric, or different bag system we are always keen on trying to mold the bag you desire. Don't be afraid to ask about something creative. We are just as interested as you are.
